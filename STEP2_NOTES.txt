GeoFPS v0.2 Terrain Step 2

This snapshot already contains the full terrain-step-2 pipeline:

- TerrainImporter loads CSV terrain points
- GeoConverter converts lat/lon/height to local X/Y/Z meters
- TerrainMeshBuilder builds a terrain mesh from geographic points
- Application loads startup terrain and rebuilds the mesh

You should not need to manually copy/paste code from chat.
Just replace your project with this folder or compare it against your local project.
